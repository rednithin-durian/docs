Durianpay enables you to create Virtual Accounts to pay to, for each of your customers. 

Once a customer pays to his virtual account, you will be notified of the payment using a webhook.
And by using the Virtual Account ID present in the webhook, you will know which customer of yours paid you.

> For more details on webhooks [click here](https://durianpay.id/docs/api/virtual-accounts/webhooks/).
